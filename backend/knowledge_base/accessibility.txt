ACCESSIBILITY GUIDE - THE GRAND HOTEL

ENTRANCE & PARKING:
- The main entrance features automated sliding doors (width 42 inches) and a gentle 1:12 slope ramp.
- Valet parking is complimentary for guests with disability placards.
- 4 designated accessible parking spots are located closest to the lobby.

ROOM FEATURES (ACCESSIBLE DELUXE):
- We have 4 dedicated 'Accessible Deluxe' rooms on the 1st floor.
- Doors: All doorways are 36 inches wide.
- Bathroom: Roll-in showers with fold-down seats, grab bars on all walls, and lowered sink counters.
- Bedroom: Bed height is 22 inches for easy transfer. Emergency pull cords are located by the bed and in the bathroom.

VISUAL & AUDITORY AIDS:
- Fire alarms feature strobe lights.
- Door knock sensors (flashing light notification) are available upon request at the front desk.
- Service animals are welcome with no additional fee.
