CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY - THE GRAND HOTEL

STANDARD BOOKINGS:
- Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before check-in (3:00 PM local time).
- Cancellations made within 48 hours of arrival will incur a fee equal to 1 night's room and tax.
- No-shows will be charged the full amount of the reservation.

PREPAID / NON-REFUNDABLE RATES:
- Bookings marked as "Advance Purchase" are non-refundable and non-changeable upon booking.
- In cases of documented medical emergencies, exceptions may be made at the General Manager's discretion (documentation required).

EARLY DEPARTURE:
- Guests checking out earlier than their scheduled departure date must notify the front desk by 11:00 AM on the day of early departure to avoid an early departure fee of $50.
